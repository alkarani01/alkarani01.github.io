Reviewing Scientific Document

Scientific manuscripts include articles for publication in a journal, proposals for funding agencies, contents, reviews, reports, forms etc. Descriptive, critical, analytical and persuasive articles need an external reviewer to check for its flow, accuracy and meaningfulness of content. Reviewing improves the quality and readability of the manuscript.

Developing Project Ideas

An idea can easily become a hypothesis. However, planning a project to check the hypothesis requires thorough thinking to design the study, select appropriate objectives and methodologies. Expert advice is needed to understand the feasibility and translational ability of the project. Many aspects of a project need to be discussed before calling it a proposal.

Designing of Figures & Illustrations

A good picture speaks a thousand words. Illustrations and diagrams are essential part of research as they help in visualizing complex ideas in an easier way. Designing a good figure includes complete understanding of the underlying concept. Its very easy to locate any missing link or mistakes from a figure. To observe and design a figure is an art. It is always a good idea to review the figure from as many viewers as possible.

Analysing Primary & Secondary Research Data

Data generated from primary and secondary research needs to be cleaned, analysed and interpreted to understand the results. Biostatistical tools are required to handle these data with care. There are many important factors that can affect the results from the same set of data if not considered during the analysis. These factors need to be selected as per the hypothesis and study design. This is the crucial part of the research and needs many trails and errors to come to a conclusion.

Discussing Life Science Topics

Any kind of scientific work needs through discussion and expert advice. Sometimes extraction of information from a complex article needs breaking it down into simpler forms. An expert helps in reducing the time to simplify and gain clarity. Further, there are issues which needs advice to find a solution or understand a process to reach to a solution. Brainstorming is an integral part of research in every step of work.
